SQL Analysis – Bicycle Store

📌 Project Overview

This project involved the complete design and analysis of a bicycle store management system using SQL. The scope included customer management, product inventory, orders, repairs, and employee scheduling.

📈 Key Features

  • 10 interrelated tables covering customers, orders, employees, repairs, and inventory.
  • SQL constraints including `CHECK`, `REGEXP`, `DEFAULT`, and `FOREIGN KEY` references.
  • Use of advanced SQL: `VIEW`, `CTE`, `WINDOW FUNCTIONS`, and aggregations.
  • Data quality validations for emails, phone numbers, and order constraints.

🧠 Insights

We generated views and analytical queries to extract customer spending patterns, service appointments, and sales distribution by item type. These helped identify high-value customers and optimize business operations.
